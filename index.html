Bienvenidos a El San Jose

Nestled in the heart of Lake City, South Carolina, El San Jose brings the rich, bold flavors of Mexico straight to your table. From sizzling fajitas to handcrafted enchiladas, every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Whether you're joining us for a family dinner, a quick lunch, or a festive celebration, our warm atmosphere and authentic cuisine will make you feel right at home.
